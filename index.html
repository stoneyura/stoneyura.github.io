OCEANO 2023

About Us

“Progressing into the Blue Economy with Sustainable Approach to Embrace Indonesia as Global Maritime Fulcrum”

Ocean Engineering Exhibition and Competition (OCEANO) is an annual event hosted by Ocean Engineering students of Sepuluh Nopember Institute of Technology intended to improve the event management skills of Ocean Engineering students and also to promote Ocean Engineering majors to society, especially high school students.
