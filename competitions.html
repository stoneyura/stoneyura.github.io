Competitions

Competition is the main event of oceano 2023. There are four categories of competition this year that undergraduate and high school students can join. ORDC, RECSC, PIC, and OSO. Each of the competitions has its own regulations, and there will be many prizes for the winners.

Renewable Energy Case Study Competition

Renewable Energy Case Study Competition is a competition where participants from all university around Indonesia are demonstrate their critical thinking abilities, determination to address complex issues and competence to work in teams in order to solve Renewable Energy-related cases.

More

Paper and Innovation Competitions

Paper and Innovation Competition is a competition for undergraduate and vocational students all over Indonesia to enhance their ability to bring new, intriguing, sustainable and eco-friendly innovations in maritime blue economy sector.

More

Oil Rig Desain Competition

Oil rig design competition is a competition that challenges participants tocombine engineering sense and creative innovations to design a semi-submersible oil rig with a specified study case.

More

Highschool Olimpics

Highschool Olympics is a competition dedicated for high school students from various schools that take the form of olympics. The olympics will consist of SNBT-standard and Ocean Engineering related questions. Highschool olympics aims to help students prepare for national university selection tests while also introducing them to the Ocean Engineering Department. Thiscompetition will be followed by an Ocean Engineering tour.
